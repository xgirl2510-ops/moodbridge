# Privacy Policy - MoodBridge

**Last updated: February 2026**

## Introduction

MoodBridge ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

---

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information

We may collect the following personal information:

- **Account Information:** Email address or phone number used for registration
- **Profile Information:** Display name, avatar (optional)
- **Mood Data:** Daily check-in mood status (happy/sad), optional notes

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data:** App features used, interaction times, crash reports
- **Analytics:** Anonymized usage patterns to improve the app

### Information from Other Users

- **Messages:** Encouragement messages you send or receive
- **Reactions:** Your reactions to received messages

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

1. **Provide Services:** Match users for encouragement, deliver messages
2. **Personalize Experience:** Show relevant content, track your progress
3. **Send Notifications:** Alert you of new messages, reminders
4. **Improve the App:** Analyze usage patterns, fix bugs
5. **Ensure Safety:** Detect and prevent abuse, enforce community guidelines
6. **Communicate:** Send important updates about the service

---

## Information Sharing

We do **NOT** sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share information in these limited circumstances:

### With Other Users
- Your anonymous ID and mood status (if you choose to receive encouragement)
- Messages you send (to the recipient only)
- Your public profile (only if you choose to be public)

### With Service Providers
- Firebase (Google) for authentication, database, and hosting
- Analytics providers (anonymized data only)

### For Legal Reasons
- To comply with legal obligations
- To protect our rights and safety
- To prevent fraud or abuse

---

## Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures including:

- Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Secure authentication (Firebase Auth)
- Access controls and monitoring
- Regular security reviews

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

1. **Access:** Request a copy of your personal data
2. **Correction:** Update or correct your information
3. **Deletion:** Request deletion of your account and data
4. **Opt-out:** Disable notifications or data collection features
5. **Portability:** Receive your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, contact us at: **privacy@moodbridge.app**

---

## Data Retention

- **Account Data:** Retained until you delete your account
- **Mood History:** Retained for 1 year, then anonymized
- **Messages:** Retained for 90 days after both users delete
- **Analytics:** Anonymized and aggregated, no retention limit

---

## Children's Privacy

MoodBridge is not intended for children under 12 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 12. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 12, please contact us immediately.

---

## International Data Transfers
